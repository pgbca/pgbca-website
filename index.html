About Us

Inspired by our notable tradition bay-le/baile, PGBCA STRONG GROUP ASSOCIATION INC envisions an assembly of united generations formed with enthusiasm and fostered by diverse cultures partaking in a remarkable dance-party-like experience led by a strong, stable, and loyal association.

Our Mission

To preserve the town's tradition where people of united generations meet and jive together to express jubilation and further develop strong camaraderie by nurturing inclusion, diversity, respect, and social awareness through the joy of dance.
